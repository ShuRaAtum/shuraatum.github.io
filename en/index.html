about

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Information and Computer Engineering at Hansung University, advised by Prof. Hwa-Jeong Seo in the CryptoCraft Lab. My work centers on platform-optimized cryptographic implementation — taking algorithms designed on paper and squeezing real performance out of ARM, RISC-V, and GPU hardware.

Recent focus is post-quantum cryptography (PQC) benchmarking and engineering across the KpqC and NIST PQC submissions, including hybrid TLS 1.3, joint-PKI infrastructure, and lightweight target platforms (Cortex-M, RV32). I also work on GPU-accelerated symmetric ciphers (CUDA, Apple Silicon Metal) and AI-agent-assisted workflows for cryptographic benchmarking and test-harness generation.

Publications: 9 SCI(E), 8 KCI, 3 preprints, with conference papers at ICISC, PlatCon, and Korean cryptography venues. See the publications page for the full list.
