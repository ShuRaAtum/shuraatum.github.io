A clean-room benchmarking suite for evaluating Korean Post-Quantum Cryptography (KpqC) Round 2
candidates on a level playing field. Provides standardized timing harnesses, memory-usage instrumentation,
and side-by-side comparisons across reference and optimized implementations.

Co-authored with Min-Joo Sim et al. Preprint on the Cryptology ePrint Archive (2024).
